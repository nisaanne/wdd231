About Yuba City

Yuba City offers a variety of beautiful sites and attractions. From natural beauty at the Buttes to cultural landmarks like the Bok Kai Temple. Ellis Lake provides a serene environment for relaxation, while the Gauche Aquatic Park is perfect for family fun. The Nagar Kirtan parade and the charming shops on Plumas Street highlight the city's vibrant community spirit.

New Business

Yuba City is welcoming several new businesses this year including Cane's Chicken, Dutch Brothers, and a career development center.

Children's Activities

The Playzeum offers interactive chilren's exhibits and an outdoor playground. Special children's events are hosted throughout the year.

Yuba city has 18 parks located throughout the town.
